Embiid Shows Off in Third Preseason Game

Joel Embiid finished the 76ers third preseason game against the Cavs with a statline of 12 points on 4-10 shooting, along with 4 rebounds. Embiid also shot 1-2 from three. The eye test shows much more than the statline does however. Embiid got his first bucket on a monster dunk that sent Sixers fans into a frenzy. Embiid later connected on a three after trailing point gaurd TJ McConnel. Embiid showed his confidence in his range and it sure looks like head coach Brett Brown has given him the green light. Embiid botched an easy easy alley oop attempt from McConnel too, something he'll more than likely work out with more playing time and experience. All together, this was a good effort from Embiid, showing off his immpressive athleticism and range. If he can stay healthy, Sixers fans will be in for a fun season.
